# Access & Quota

PulsWeb2 **does not issue a token**. Access to features may require approval or a quota, depending on your environment.

Depending on the current stage (private test / internal preview), you may need **access approval** or a **usage quota** to run workloads.

### 1. Get Access
- Join the community and request access through the official channel (e.g. Discord / form / invitation).
- Once approved, your account will be able to deploy Confidential Services and run Confidential Tasks.

### 2. Understand What You Pay With (Current State)
- If you see a wallet connection in the UI, it is used for **identity and signing** (proving you are the owner of a workload).
- Billing / quota / payment rails may change across releases. This doc will only describe what is confirmed in the codebase.

### 3. What You Need Before Trying
- A supported wallet (if required by the dApp build you are using).
- Your account approved / quota enabled.

---

If your team already has a staging environment, ask the admin for the correct onboarding steps (endpoint, network, and access rules).
